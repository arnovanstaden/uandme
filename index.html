The Venue

Lace on Timber is an industrial wedding and event venue just outside of Pretoria that can accommodate from 50 up to 200 people.

Wedding ceremonies can be held among 100-year-old trees with gutted old doors as backdrop, while the guests are seated on wooden benches. The reception area is decorated with chandeliers and festive bulb lights for a touch of glamour.
